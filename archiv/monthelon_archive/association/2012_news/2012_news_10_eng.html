NEWSLETTER Octobre 2012
 
Administrative:

Notable points evoked at the last counsil of the administration meeting on October 14 were:
-the possibility of organizing several gala performances to fund Monthelon
-that from now on, each artist who comes to do a residence will be invited to adhere to the association which will activate their being covered by Monthelon's insurance as well as their support of the association
-a day for the exchange of ideas and thoughts concerning Monthelon will be organized this year
-the provisional budget has been established in order to enable us to concentrate on hiring an administrator
-the next "rencontres" is being thought over carefully.
You will receive a more detailed summary soon.
-a subvention for funding for a new heating system has been granted!….the other parts of the funding still have to be found.
-Camille, the administrator for Anomalie worked with Ueli and they deposited several other requests for funding
-the report of activities is in the process of being formed.
-the office caravan is being repaired to better receive our new administrator, when we hire him
-Ayin's contract "aide" has been prolonged for another 6 months.
