Association du Château de Monthelon
NEWSLETTER AUGUST/SEPTEMBER 2012
 
Administrative:

After our last general assembly meeting on the 3Oth of August, it seems necessary to reaffirm the idea that Monthelon's commitment is to the invention and development of ideas.
To this end (and for the permanent renewal of this adventure), all of the desires for artistic activities and other ambitions for projects, must be considered. As a whole, the general administration, in collaboration with its artistic director Ueli Hirzel, is engaged, to the best of its capacities, to the study of all the propositions to come.

For the sake of memory, the members of the administrative council are:
Sylvie Cadenat, Laurence Guillot, Ayin de Sela, Daniel Ott, Sky de Sela, Andrès Tapia, Adell Nodé-Langlois, AnnTanguy, Jean Benoit Mollet, Delphine Lanson, Adeline Morin, Cille Lansade.

A lively discussion about the latest rencontres took place. A summary of the reactions of the members of the administration will be shared ( your reactions are also very welcome).

The next meeting of the administrative council will be on the 14th of October.

The forms for the "Heating Subvention" are deposited but there remains 70, 000 euros to be found.
