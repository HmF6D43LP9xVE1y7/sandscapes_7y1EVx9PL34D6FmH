Monthelon is above all a PLACE; with a geographical , historical, stone reality!
It is a place where things can happen. It is a place that welcomes artists in residency.The only thing that is asked of the artist in return, is to Pay attention to Monthelon. Like the physical law, of equal and opposite force, the more you are close to it, the more you give it, the more it gives back, the more is possible. These are the times that Monthelon opens completely and allows astounding research and exploration to take place.

Monthelon is committed to research, to creativity, to the developement and realisation of ideas (artsitic, political, economic and social),  to the exchange of these creative experiments and finally, to the opening up of entirely new ways.

sc/ue 2008translated by Sky de Sela
