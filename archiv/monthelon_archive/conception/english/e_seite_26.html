Sharing these experiences  can be encouraging. Sometimes it takes only  an exchange, a few words with another artist, to get past that blocked spot, to take that one step that takes us further, instead of running in mad circles around ourselves, in poor, panicky imitation of our own creative selves.

WHAT ARE THE CONDITIONS NECESSARY TO A CREATIVE PROCESS?

Desire, perserverance, courage, rigor and fragility are indespensable.
To go into the creative process is to leave behind so much...start from zero. If we know where we're going, then we haven't actually left anything.
Taking time with yourself, a lot of time, accepting to explore your own limits.
