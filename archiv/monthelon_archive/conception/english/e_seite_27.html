WHAT CONTEXT IS HELPFUL TO ENCOURAGE AND SUSTAIN THE CREATIVE PROCESS?

One that will permit the artist to quit the everyday life for a given amount of time, to change  habits, to leave behind the material worries that distract and consume. To be surrounded by people who are going through a similar process and to have the possibility of exchanging  on the differences and similarities of thet process.
Silence, nature, an immense sky and limitless horizon...
As well as technical support, space, construction possibilities, help building.
Last but not least, the possibility to relax!
