We  have dived in here, without defenses, and with no hope of being elsewhere, no relief in sight.
Uncomfortable as we are, we begin to feel at home here.
What is difficult to us changes color, a word, a taste, a tone, a gesture, a mouvement...
Inspiration comes one drop at a time,
Intuition becomes visible.

Ueli Hirzel, january 2008
