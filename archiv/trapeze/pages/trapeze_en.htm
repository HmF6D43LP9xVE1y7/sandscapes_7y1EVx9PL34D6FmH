     Project of a stage-play suspended from the air

Un horizonte cuadrado

It is a human story, based on personal experiences. A quest into the human condition, and the  futile struggle  to change it. It is about the impossibility to be, and also to not be, with the other. Or is it simply an aerial choreography, a living, breathing space in the air with all the emotion, beauty and tenderness suspended above the precipice? Different lectures are possible.

The Trapeze

... is a little metal tube suspended in the air by two ropes. 
The trapeze artists are those beings that live on the trapezes.
They live in their own world in the air. This world has its own laws, limits and possibilities. 
This world is invisible for us terrestrials. 
By chance, under certain circumstances, this world with its delicate moments become visible to us, and may provide us very particular experiences.
